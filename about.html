THOMAS CARLSON . EXECUTIVE CHEF

As the executive chef of The Westwood Grill, Thomas Carlson has firmly established himself as one of the city's most talented and decorated culinary masters. Carlson's culinary forte lies in his masterful fusion of global cuisines, seamlessly blending international flavors and techniques into creative, genre-defying dishes that are both visually stunning and explosively flavorful. His dedication to locally sourced, sustainable ingredients is evidenced through his strong relationships with regional farmers and purveyors. Carlson's innovative vegetable-forward tasting menus have earned him widespread acclaim for elevating plant-based cuisine to new artistic heights. Among his impressive accolades are a coveted Michelin star, a James Beard Award for Best Chef, and victory on the Iron Chef television competition. Carlson has also published two highly praised cookbooks, sharing his unique culinary perspective and signature recipes with home cooks worldwide. Beyond the kitchen, he is an active philanthropist, generously giving back to community organizations that provide culinary training for underprivileged youth.

MICHELINE ROUSSEAU . GENERAL MANAGER

Micheline Rousseau is the esteemed General Manager of The Delicacy, an award-winning boutique hotel celebrated for its exceptional gastronomic experiences. With over two decades of expertise in luxury hospitality, Rousseau has carved out a reputation as an authority in curating world-class culinary offerings and unparalleled service. Her sharp business acumen is evidenced through The Delicacy's consistent profitability, driven by strategic revenue management practices and her talent for developing creative revenue streams. Rousseau's true passion, however, lies in her relentless pursuit of culinary perfection. She has cultivated long-standing partnerships with renowned chefs, vintners, and artisanal purveyors to ensure The Delicacy's fine dining experiences feature the finest, locally-sourced ingredients presented with impeccable technique. Under her leadership, the hotel's signature restaurant has earned multiple Michelin stars and James Beard recognitions. Rousseau's dedication extends to providing guests with exclusive culinary programming, from intimate chef's table experiences to immersive wine tastings and educational cooking classes. Her ability to combine exemplary hospitality with gastronomic excellence has solidified The Delicacy's status as a world-leading culinary destination.
